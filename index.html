About

I presented these projects at the Board of Education meeting for my school district, advocating for their inclusion in the curriculum for future students. Currently, I am focusing on Web Development projects, enjoying solving challenges across the entire stack. Additionally, I am eager to explore cloud computing and have begun exploring AWS.
